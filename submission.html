Submission

Paper Types

We solicit the following types of submissions:

  • Research papers (6 pages + up to 2 pages of references)
  • Short papers (2-4 pages + up to 1 page of references)
    • Position Papers aimed at sharing the authorsâ€™ opinion about the open challenges to foster discussion;
    • Tool/Data Showcase Papers aimed at presenting tools and datasets.

Submission Format

All submissions must use IEEE templates and should be submitted through EasyChair.

Submission Instructions

As with SANER 2019,

  • All submissions must come in PDF format and conform, at time of submission, to the IEEE Conference Proceedings Formatting Guidelines (title in 24pt font and full text in 10pt font, LaTeX users must use \documentclass[10pt,conference]{IEEEtran} without including the compsoc or compsocconf option. Also, papers must comply with the IEEE Policy on Authorship.
  • Our workshop follows a full double-blind review process. For the detailed instructions that the submitted manuscripts must follow, please see the Instructions for Authors.
